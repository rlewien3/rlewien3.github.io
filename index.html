Pet Projects

NaviGloves

I designed and created a working prototype of smart cycling gloves, assisting cyclists to navigate safely on their rides.

They link to Google Maps to gradually warn the cyclist of upcoming turns in their route, and flash bright red lights to signal when turning.

GazeHelp Photoshop Plugin

I integrated eye tracking features into Adobe Photoshop, designing and coding a custom plugin to deliver three features: QuickTool, X-Ray and Privacy Shield.

Diabetes Tracker Webapp

This webapp was designed and developed to let patients track their daily diabetes measurements, and clinicians to manage them. This was completed as part of INFO30023, using Node, Express, Handlebars, vanilla CSS and MongoDB.
