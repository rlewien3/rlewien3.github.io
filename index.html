Hi! I'm Ryan.

UX Designer and Developer

I’m Ryan Lewien, a digital creative working as a UX designer at NAB. With a background in software engineering through my Master of IT (HCI), I develop intuitive, pixel-perfect user interfaces based on a thorough understanding of users' needs.

Pet Projects

ESNLC Website

I fully redesigned and developed the Elwood St Kilda Neighbourhood Learning Centre’s website, including a new logo and a custom theme.

Over several months I worked closely with the team to determine requirements and maximise effectiveness for the centre long-term. I also made special care to simplify upkeep, with all page categories automatically updating after any new posts are created.

WordPress • PHP • Javascript • HTML • CSS

Erbe Project

This web app was created for a client family to store & pass down heritage items and stories.

I completed this with a team of four other students, mainly focusing on the development of the front end but also utilising advanced technologies such as the Google Vision API for automatic tagging of images into categories.

Pug • CSS • Bootstrap • Material Design NodeJS • Express • MongoDB

Officeworks

Bourke St Bushfire Appeal

I was tasked with creating all signage for the Bourke St Officeworks fundraising appeal that specifically raised towards the Australian Bushfire relief. This included banners, posters, signage for items being sold as well as voucher cards.

The project was great experience in creating from a design spec, and aligning with the greater Officeworks visual look.

Photoshop • Figma • Illustrator
