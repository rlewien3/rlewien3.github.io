Hi! I'm Ryan Lewien.

Software Developer, IS Masters Student & UX Enthusiast.

About Me

I’m Ryan, a Master of Information Systems student from Melbourne, Australia who enjoys building polished software for the web and beyond. With a background in computing and software systems and a strong passion for design, I develop intuitive, pixel-perfect user interfaces with efficient and modern backends.

Here’s a few technologies I’ve been working with recently:

  • Javascript (ES6+)
  • HTML & (S)CSS
  • Java
  • PHP
  • Node.js
  • Express
  • Pug

What I do

Development

Software development, both front and back end, are what I enjoy and do best.

Developing for the web is my bread and butter, but through a Bachelor of Science (Computing & Software Systems) I’ve been extending my tech stack to wider software systems.

Design

What is software if the users don't enjoy working with it?

I have a strong background in UX and visual design, both through my Masters specialisation in Human-Computer Interaction, pet projects and bootcamps. I’m all about the “clean yet beautiful”!

Involvement

I’m proud of the impact I’ve had on the University and wider community.

Currently acting as President of the Melbourne University Choral Society and Fundraising Officer for the University's Save the Children Club, I also volunteer in my spare time doing web development for the ESNLC Community Centre.
